## How to Obtain Access

1. **Purchase a licence** (see pricing in the [main README](https://github.com/AI-CrownStar-AI-BOT/CrownStar_AI_Bot)).
2. **Email** `downloads@crownstar.ai` with your purchase receipt and GitHub username.
3. You will be added as a collaborator to the relevant private repository.
4. Download your installer from the private repo’s **Releases** section.
